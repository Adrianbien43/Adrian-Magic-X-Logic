Magic X Logic

Magic X Logic is a universe where magic and scientific logic coexist in a fragile and often conflicting balance. This world is populated by factions that represent both forces:

Magic

In Magic X Logic, magic is the pure energy emanating from the Eternal Bond, a multiversal being that connects all dimensions and living beings. The Battle Angels channel this magic through a special bond with magical spirits, entities that represent various aspects of cosmic power.

Logic

In Magic X Logic, Logic is a force derived from the knowledge of the Locrosf, an ancient race that mastered the fundamental laws of the universe. The Philosophers, warriors of logic, use this power through a system of “pure logic” passed down by the Locrosf, allowing them to manipulate reality based on scientific and mathematical principles.

LATEST

The existence of fear of the unknown gives life to a formidable rival.

Crown of Thorns, supreme leader of the Philosophers, is a figure shrouded in mystery, marked by a past known to few. His mastery of the enigmatic God Particles allows him to transcend planes and rewrite the laws of the universe. Once linked to magic, he now pursues a hidden purpose, guided only by the cold precision of logic. His presence inspires both fear and reverence among followers and enemies alike.
