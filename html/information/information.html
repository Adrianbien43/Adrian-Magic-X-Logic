Purpose

"Magic X Logic Universe"

With Magic X Logic, I seek to challenge the boundaries of imagination and expand storytelling possibilities across multiple formats: books, stories, comics, films, series, and video games. My goal is to immerse the audience in a universe full of mystery, adventure, and emotion, where each medium will offer a unique and immersive experience. This saga not only explores the tension between magic and logic, but also invites reflection on the nature of balance, power, and the connection between seemingly opposing worlds.

Introduction

"Magic X Logic Universe"

Magic X Logic was born from the imagination of a creator inspired by the duality of forces that govern the cosmos: the mystical and the rational. The main idea is to seek the fusion of the mystical and the scientific in a unique narrative. A universe full of complex characters, futuristic settings, and deep questions about the nature of power and balance.

Biography

"Magic X Logic Universe"

I am passionate about the intersection between the fantastical and the rational — a point where magic and science converge in a delicate balance, as fascinating as it is dangerous. How could pure logic coexist with a force as uncontrollable as magic? Is it possible for both forces to live together without canceling each other out?
