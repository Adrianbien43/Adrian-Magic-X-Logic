THE CHARACTERS OF MAGIC X LOGIC

THESE WILL BE THE CHARACTERS APPEARING IN THE FIRST CHAPTERS

MAGIC X LOGIC will begin with key characters that will shape this universe of fantasy, science, and fiction. Each of these characters will share their personal stories through different narratives in which they will grow and evolve. Later on, their paths will cross in new stories, creating a network of interconnected adventures.

Nyxiel

Nyxiel, a battle angel in a cyberpunk city, discovers that his dark past is linked to the chaos he seeks to eliminate. On his mission to stop three philosophers and their leader, Crown of Thorns, he must face his own identity and the conflict between magic and logic. The battle for truth and balance will test his soul and destiny.

Arioth

On a tidal moon dominated by space pirates, Arioth, the battle angel with the power of chains, must face Blackbeard, a rogue philosopher who manipulates tides and trapped souls. In a hostile environment of chaos and war, Arioth will battle not only his enemy but also the dark truths of his own power. The battle for control of the moon will test his morality and the fate of its inhabitants.

Selene

The third battle angel, Zariel, possesses flavor magic, which grants her extraordinary powers by consuming different tastes: superhuman strength, agility, speed, invisibility, and more. Her story begins on a rocky moon surrounded by towering mountain ranges, where she faces a philosopher who, though defeating her, awakens a new determination in her. Defeat will only drive her to discover her magic's true potential and destiny.

Solara

Solara, the battle angel with the power of a neutron star, defends a drifting space city threatened by a philosopher planning a cosmic genocide. To stop him, she will gather the other battle angels for a mission to save the innocent. The fight will be an epic battle for the survival and balance of the universe.

Eryndor

Eryndor, a battle angel who can change personalities and enhance his attributes, seeks to transform a gloomy gothic city. One of his personalities allows him to manipulate logic, giving him a strategic edge. However, he faces the mayor, a philosopher who controls people through knowledge, preventing Eryndor from fulfilling his mission.

Metamorphosys

Metamorphosys is a philosopher who transforms his body into monstrous animals to spread terror and manipulate people, inciting genocides against innocent creatures. His power destabilizes worlds, and only a battle angel can stop him.

Catalyst

Catalyst is a philosopher with the power to turn everything he touches into a deadly bomb. He uses his ability to carry out attacks on the magical organization, spreading chaos and weakening its key members. His goal is to destroy from within, destabilizing the balance between magic and logic.

Abaddon

This philosopher, known as "Abaddon," was a respected scientist obsessed with achieving godlike power. After experimenting on his own body, he lost all humanity, becoming an emotionless, amoral abomination. Now, under the control of Crown of Thorns, he is her feared "lapdog," a weapon of destruction executing deadly missions to hunt and eliminate battle angels. His lack of humanity makes him an unstoppable machine capable of fulfilling any mission without remorse.

Vespera

Mordrath is the right hand of Crown of Thorns, a philosopher feared for his blood magic and logic capable of bending space at will. With terrifying ease, he can annihilate a battle angel, and his power makes him an unstoppable threat. His connection with darkness and chaos makes him a feared figure, executing merciless missions and destroying everything in his path.

Pinella

Pinella is a living puppet made from the parts of an extinct race, controlled by higher beings and one of the most loyal members of Crown of Thorns. With a psychopathic nature, she enjoys torturing and killing her victims, especially battle angels, whom she hunts without mercy. Her existence is an aberration, a mix of macabre engineering and pure evil, making her an unstoppable and terrifying threat.
